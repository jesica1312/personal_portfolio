About

I'm Jesica

Hi, I'm Jesica Patel, a data analyst with a strong foundation in IT and a passion for leveraging data to drive meaningful insights. With experience spanning data analysis, IT support, and technical consulting, I specialize in working with SQL, Excel, and data visualization tools to support decision-making in dynamic environments.


I hold a Master’s in Information Technology Management from California Baptist University and a Bachelor’s in Information and Communication Technology from DA-IICT. My professional experience includes working as a Technical Analyst, where I collaborated with stakeholders to gather requirements, perform data analysis, and develop dashboards to support business strategies. More recently, as a Data Analyst at Rogers, Anderson, Melody & Scott LLP, I supported technology operations, ensuring efficient data management and troubleshooting systems to enhance workflow efficiency.


My passion lies in using data to solve real-world problems, particularly in the education and social impact domains. I am currently working on projects that analyze online learning platform performance and explore data-driven strategies for improving user engagement and learning outcomes.


I thrive in analytical roles that require critical thinking, attention to detail, and effective storytelling through data visualization. I’m always looking for opportunities to refine my skills, work with diverse datasets, and contribute to data-driven decision-making.
